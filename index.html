Process

Concept to final design.

I like the full design path: understanding the idea, blocking out the structure, solving the messy parts, optimizing the model, and preparing the final presentation.

01

Research & Concept

Study references, identify the main mechanical forms, and decide what the model needs to communicate visually.

02

Blockout & Structure

Build the main volumes first, test proportions, organize parts, and make sure the system is readable before adding detail.

03

Modeling & Iteration

Refine hard-surface forms, pipe routes, repeated components, materials, and layout while solving scale and clarity problems.

04

Optimization & Final Presentation

Clean up the asset, improve performance where needed, adjust materials and lighting, then use rendering or Photoshop to finish the visual.
